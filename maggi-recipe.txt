Ingredients:
 - 1 packet Maggi
 - Salt
 - Black Pepper
 - Tabasco Sauce
 - 1 Frozen Mix Vegetable
 - Water

Directions:
 1. Boil the frozen vegetables until they become soft.
 2. Add Maggi, vegetables and sachet of maggi in water and boil them.
 3. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
 4. Add 7 drops Tabasco sauce.
 5. Stir them well until all the water is evaporated.
